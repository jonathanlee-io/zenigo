Frequently asked questions

What is Zenigo.io?

Zenigo.io is a user feedback platform that allows you to embed a lightweight feedback widget directly into your website with a single HTML script tag. It helps you gather actionable insights from your visitors to improve your product.

How do I add the feedback widget to my website?

Adding the feedback widget is as simple as copying and pasting a one-line HTML script tag into your website’s code. The widget integrates seamlessly and requires no additional setup.

What kind of insights can I gather with Zenigo.io?

Zenigo.io helps you collect detailed feedback from users, including ratings, comments, and feature suggestions. It also provides real-time insights and analytics to help you understand your customers’ needs better.
